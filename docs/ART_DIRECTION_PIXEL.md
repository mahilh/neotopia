## 🏗️ MASTER STYLE BLOCK (copy-paste at start of EVERY prompt)

```
Background: deep navy blue (#0A0A2E) · square 1:1 · NO gradients · NO landscape · no other buildings
Stone: warm amber-gold brick texture · octagonal or circular main structure
Crystal: teal (#40E0D0) glow on windows · dome · accent spires
Entrance: gothic arch doorway with teal glow + stone steps leading up
Dome: teal crystal glass-panel dome at building apex with golden ring finial
Corner accents: 4 small teal crystal pyramid spires at base corners
Ground: grey stone diamond platform (6x6 tiles) · nothing else surrounding
Building height: fills 70% of image height · centered
Style: 16-bit pixel art · SNES isometric · 12-16 color palette · crisp pixel edges
NO anti-aliasing · NO gradients within elements · NO painterly strokes
```
