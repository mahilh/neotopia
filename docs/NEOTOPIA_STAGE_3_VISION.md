## STAGE 2 → STAGE 3 TRANSITION

Stage 2 (NeoTopia.io) ends when the first shovel goes in the ground of Stage 3. Until then, every game played is rehearsal. Every card scored is a vote for which buildings get built first.

The Global Civilization Index measures this. When enough games have been played that the same cards appear most frequently in top scores, those cards identify which buildings the civilization wants first. The index is a democratic oracle.

**Stage 3 trigger conditions:**
  - The Global Index has received games from at least 100 unique players
  - The top three most-scored cards across all games have been identified
  - A physical location has been found for the first district
  - Austin, TX (post-July 2026 relocation) is the Stage 3 candidate city

## WHAT STAGE 3 LOOKS LIKE

### Physical district design principles:
  Same four elements as the game: Energy · BioFarming · Technology · Community
  Same three-region structure: each region specializes in its element
  The cluster bonus made physical: buildings close together share resources (district heating, shared garden, data infrastructure)

### The first building: whatever card scores highest in the Index
  If Holographic Research Center (card_33) leads: build a community science space
  If Food Forest (card_20) leads: start with the garden
  If Free Energy Lab (card_23) leads: build the research facility
  The civilization tells you what it wants first through the game

### The scoring formula made real:
  Best + Second + (Worst×3) + Cluster
  In a real district: the weakest element needs 3× the investment of the strongest
  Community is the element most districts neglect (worst×3 forces this into focus)
  Cluster: buildings physically adjacent share infrastructure (literal holographic coherence)
