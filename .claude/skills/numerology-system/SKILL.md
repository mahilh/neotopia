## TESLA'S KEY: 3 · 6 · 9 = THE UNIVERSE
  NeoTopia is built on these numbers:
  3 regions · 3 factories · 3 actions per turn · worst region ×3

## KEY NUMBER MEANINGS FOR GAME DESIGN
  2 = duality (2pt cards = first relationships)
  3 = trinity/creation (3pt cards = synthesis)
  4 = earth/stability (4pt cards = material foundation)
  5 = life/bridge (5pt cards = spirit↔matter)
  7 = spiritual perfection (score threshold #1)
  9 = completion (35+36 placed=9, Council of Nine=9, everything returns to 9)
  11 = illumination (56 cards = 5+6=11, the Master Intuition number)
  12 = cosmic order (12 production tiles = 12 cosmic cycles)
  13 = transformation (score threshold #2 = sacred feminine)
  18 = life doubled (score threshold #3 = 1+8=9=completion)
  22 = master builder (card_22 Sound Frequency Gateway)
  33 = master teacher (card_33 Holographic Research Center, grandfather's number)

## SCORE MILESTONE CELEBRATIONS
  At total scores 9, 18, 27, 36: show sacred flash + brief notification
  At regional scores 7, 13, 18: show score-specific sacred symbol overlay (0.8s)
  These milestones are never announced to players — they are discovered

## MASTER NUMBER CARDS (implement special frame someday)
  card_11, card_22, card_33, card_44, card_55: all master numbers
  These should have slightly different frame treatment (larger border glow?)

## COSMIC ALIGNMENT BONUS (optional mechanic)
  If all 3 regional scores sum to multiple of 9: award +3 bonus
  This rewards balance over maxing one region
  This is the "Cosmic Alignment" achievement

## FIBONACCI IN BOARD LAYOUT
  3 factories = triangle = 3 (Fibonacci)
  4 elements = the 4th Fibonacci position
  ~19 hexes per region = closest to 21 (Fibonacci)
  The board is a Fibonacci organism
